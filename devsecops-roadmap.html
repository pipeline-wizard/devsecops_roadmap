Deloitte-Specific Tips

Use Deloitte University (DU) and internal learning platforms — many Microsoft certs have subsidized vouchers or free attempts through the partnership.
Volunteer for client engagements involving Azure security or AI — real project hours count more than lab time and you get paid to learn.
Log certifications in DTTL Skills Profiler and update your Staffing Profile — managers use this for project staffing decisions.
Mornings before your noon shift are golden — study when your brain is fresh. Don't try to study after a 10 PM logout.
Build buffer weeks before exams. Consulting is unpredictable — client escalations happen. Every phase here has 1–2 weeks of slack built in.
Connect with the Cyber & Strategic Risk practice internally — they run study groups and have architects who mentor for AZ-500 and SC-100.
