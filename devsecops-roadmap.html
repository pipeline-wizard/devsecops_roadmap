🏢 Deloitte-Specific Tips

Check Deloitte's Enterprise Subscription Agreement (ESA) with Microsoft — many certs have subsidized or free vouchers. Ask your People Leader or L&D contact.
Volunteer for client engagements involving Azure security or AI. Real project hours count more than lab time, and you're getting paid to learn.
Log every certification in DTTL Skills Profiler and update your Staffing Profile immediately — managers use these for project staffing decisions.
Mornings before your noon shift are sacred study time. Study when your brain is fresh. Never try to study after a 10 PM logout — it doesn't stick.
Every phase has 1–2 buffer weeks built in for client escalations. If a phase goes smoothly, use the buffer for extra hands-on labs.
Connect with the Cyber & Strategic Risk practice internally — they run study groups and have architects who mentor for AZ-500 and SC-100.
When you finish the DevSecOps portfolio phase, propose your pipeline security templates as a Deloitte internal accelerator — great visibility with leadership.
After SC-100, you'll be positioned for Senior Consultant or a lateral move into the Cyber practice. Start that conversation around month 10.
